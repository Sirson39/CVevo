How we calculate scores

Our ATS engine scans your resume for specific technical skills, tools, and industry keywords defined in the job post.

A higher score indicates better alignment with the recruiter’s automated filters.

Understanding Your Score

Our advanced AI-powered engine evaluates your resume against industry standards and specific job descriptions to provide you with an objective score.

ATS Match Score (Targeted)

When you analyze your resume against a specific job post, we calculate the match percentage based on how many required keywords, skills, and tools from the job description are successfully identified in your resume.

Quality Score (Quick Scan)

A Quick Scan assesses the overall structural quality of your resume across critical categories like Professional Summary, Grammar & Spelling, Section Completeness, and Readability. Each category contributes to the total score of 100.

Pro Tip for Higher Scores

Tailor your resume for every application. Use the exact keywords mentioned in the job post, and ensure your formatting is clean and ATS-friendly without complex tables or graphics.
