Resume Tips that work with ATS

Practical tips to improve structure, clarity, and keywords — without making your resume look robotic.

Analyze my resume → Browse templates →

1) Use ATS-safe structure

Keep headings simple: Summary, Skills, Experience, Education. Avoid tables, columns, and heavy icons.

2) Write impact bullets

Action + What you did + Result. Add numbers if possible (%, time saved, revenue, users).

3) Keyword strategy

Use keywords from the job description naturally in Skills and Experience (tools, roles, tech, methods).

4) Keep it readable

Use consistent font sizes, spacing, and bullet style. Recruiters scan fast — don’t make it hard.

5) Proofread like a system

Fix dates, titles, spacing, and spelling. ATS parsing errors often come from inconsistent formatting.

Quick checklist

✅ One column layout
✅ Standard headings
✅ PDF or DOCX (clean export)
✅ Skills aligned with role
✅ Recent experience first
