ATS Guide: how systems read your resume

ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) scan resumes for structure and keywords. If the content can’t be parsed clearly, your score drops even if you’re qualified.

Check my ATS score → Resume tips →

What ATS usually scans

Headings, dates, job titles, company names, skills, tools, certifications, education, and keyword matches.

What ATS may ignore

Text inside images, complex tables, multi-column layouts, fancy graphics, and unusual section titles.

Best file formats

Clean PDF or DOCX. If your PDF is generated from images, ATS may fail to read the text correctly.

Keyword matching

ATS often checks overlap with job description keywords. Use the same terms where accurate (tools, skills).

Ranking & shortlisting

Recruiters may filter by score or required skills first. Clear structure helps both ATS and humans.

Safe formatting rules

• One column
• Standard fonts
• Simple bullets
• Clear headings
• Consistent dates
