Recruiter FAQs

How does the AI ranking work?
The system uses NLP to compare resume text against your job's 'ATS Scanning Keywords'. We prioritize keyword density, structural match, and specific tool proficiencies.
Is there a limit on bulk uploads?
Standard accounts can process up to 50 resumes per batch. For larger batches, the system may process in multiple segments to maintain accuracy.
Can I edit a job after posting?
Yes. Go to 'Manage Jobs' and click the job title to edit its requirements. Note that re-saving a job may trigger a recalibration of existing candidate scores.
Candidate scores seem low, why?
Ensure your 'ATS Scanning Keywords' are specific (e.g., use 'React 18' instead of just 'Web'). Broad keywords result in lower precision scores.
